BACKGROUND

This report offers key insights into the in-app programmatic advertising landscape based on in-depth analysis of the ad requests, bids, and impressions on the Smaato platform. The six topics analyzed in this report include: consumers’ growing mobile app usage, EEA inventory trends post-GDPR, mobile ad fraud, retailers’ mobile ad spending, rewarded video growth, and Cyber Monday and Singles’ Day trends. Read more at Smaato's website.
